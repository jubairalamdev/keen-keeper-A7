# 🤝 KeenKeeper

> **Helping you stay close to the people who matter.**
> A simple way to track, manage, and grow your friendships.

---

## 📖 What is KeenKeeper?

**KeenKeeper** is an easy-to-use app designed to help you be a better friend. It keeps track of when you last talked to someone so you don’t accidentally lose touch. Use it to see who you should call next and look back at your favorite social moments.

---

## ✨ Main Features

### 1. 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Friend Dashboard
See all your friends in one place. Each friend has a color-coded status:
* **On Track:** You’ve talked recently!
* **Needs Attention:** It’s been a while—maybe give them a call.

### 2. 📅 History Log (Timeline)
A simple list of every time you texted, called, or video-chatted with someone. You can filter the list to quickly find specific memories.

### 3. 📊 Simple Progress Charts
See "at a glance" how you spend your time. Do you text more than you call? Our easy charts show you the data.

## 🚀 Everything the App Can Do

### 🏠 Home Page
- **Status Bar:** Quickly see how many friends you have and how many need a "hello."
- **Friend Cards:** Each friend gets their own box with their photo and name.
- **Search & Filter:** Type a name to find a friend fast, or filter by who needs attention.

### 📅 History Page
- **Timeline:** A list of your chats from newest to oldest.
- **Filter by Type:** Only see calls, only see texts, or video chats.

### 📊 Stats Page
- **Visual Charts:** Fun graphs that show your social habits.
- **Breakdown:** See a pie chart of how you communicate most.

### 📱 Works on All Devices
- **Mobile Friendly:** The app looks great and works perfectly on your phone, tablet, or computer.
